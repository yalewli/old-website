Our Events

We hold events throughout the year to support our mission of empowering women. In the fall semester we hold our Leading Ladies' Gala, in the beginning of the spring semester we hold our Women Empowering (WE) Conference, and we bring guest speakers to Yale throughout the year as part of our Speakers and Workshops program.

Leading Ladies' Gala

Our signature event of the fall semester recognizes the achievements of outstanding undergraduate female leaders at Yale. We bring over 200 undergraduates, alumnae, faculty, and guest speakers annually to engage in a spectacular celebration of female leadership on campus and beyond.

W.E. Conference

Our annual Women Empowering (WE) Conference has, since 2006, expanded its reach to attract more than 400 student attendees from multiple universities and a vast spectrum of speakers hailing from every discipline, from finance to sports management to journalism. Past keynote speakers include Arianna Huffington, (founder of Huffington Post), Mary Robinson (President of Ireland), and Janet Robinson (the first female CEO of The New York Times Company).

Thank you for joining WLI for our 10th annual conference featuring Katherine Kolbert, Director of Barnard College Athena Center for Leadership Studies and former president of People for the American Way Foundation, who argued the landmark case of Planned Parenthood v. Casey before the U.S. Supreme Court and has been credited with saving Roe v. Wade! Katherine was joined by 50+ other women leaders on 12 panels covering every topic from being a Yale alum to performing arts careers and women in government! Our speakers included the President of the National Committee on American Foreign Policy, the Executive Director of Teach for America NY, and a member of the first Yale College graduating class to include women.

Photos of the conference will be featured here soon! In the meantime, if you have any memories, pictures, or takeaways from the conference that you would like to share, please email: Valerie Chen.
