ABOUT US

Yale Women's Leadership Initiative empowers young women to become successful leaders both on campus and in their careers. Through WLI, members find their career direction, develop their professional networks, and set and achieve ambitious career goals. WLI is now in its tenth year as an organization, and is the premier women's leadership group on campus.

Our Main Events

Founded in 2013, the annual Leading Ladies Gala is one of WLI's signature events for the fall semester. It was established to recognize the achievements of outstanding undergraduate female leaders at Yale. It aims to bring together 200+ Yale undergraduates, alumnae, faculty, and guest speakers annually to engage in a spectacular celebration of female leadership on campus and beyond.

Our annual Women Empowering (WE) Conference has, since 2006, expanded its reach to attract more than 400 student attendees from multiple universities and a vast spectrum of speakers hailing from every discipline, from finance to sports management to journalism. Past keynote speakers include Arianna Huffington, (founder of Huffington Post), Mary Robinson (President of Ireland), and Janet Robinson (the first female CEO of The New York Times Company).

We also host skill development workshops, career exposes, mentorship programs, speaker series, and more throughout the year.
